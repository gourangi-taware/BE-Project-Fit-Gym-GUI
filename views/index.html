About Us

Nowadays virtual assistant is playing a very important role in our daily activities and has become an inseparable part of our lives. As per the Clutch survey report that was published in 2019, almost 27% of people are using AI virtual assistants for performing their day-to-day activities. AI is an emerging field that we aim to explore through this project of AI-based workout assistants. In our work, we introduce Fitcercise, an application that detects the user’s exercise pose counts the specified exercise repetitions and provides personalized, detailed recommendations on how the user can improve their form. The application uses the MediaPipe to detect a person’s pose, and afterwards analyses the geometry of the pose from the dataset and real-time video and counts the repetitions of the particular exercise.
